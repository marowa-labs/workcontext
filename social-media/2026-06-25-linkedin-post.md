# LinkedIn Post

I'm building something I believe in — and I'm doing it in public.

The idea: what if your team's collective knowledge actually worked for you? Not buried in scattered docs and forgotten chats — but connected, intelligent, and always accessible.

So I'm building WorkContext.

WorkContext is an open-source, AI-powered productivity workspace that brings together:
✍️ Real-time collaborative editing (think Google Docs, for teams)
🤖 AI that understands your workspace context
✅ Smart task extraction — highlight text, create work, done
💬 Built-in team chat per project
📊 Workspace & task management
🔐 MFA auth, version history, and automatic backups

Tech stack: Next.js 16, TypeScript, PostgreSQL + pgvector, Supabase, Yjs/CRDT, Gemini & OpenAI

I rebranded it from scratch, rebuilt the entire identity, and pushed it to GitHub.

It's open source. It's free. And it's still early — I'm shipping in public so you can watch it grow and shape where it goes.

🔗 GitHub: github.com/marowa-labs/workcontext

If your team struggles with context-switching, scattered knowledge, or too many tools — this might help.

Would love feedback from the community. What would you want to see next? 🚀
