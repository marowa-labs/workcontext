# New Features

## 1. The Architecture: "The Knowledge Ecosystem"

> We are shifting from a static "library" to a dynamic **ecosystem** that moves with the user.

### Universal Knowledge Capture (Re-Purposed Intelligent Discovery)

**The Input:**

- Users drop in PDFs, meeting transcripts (from Fireflies or Otter), Slack exports, or web links.

**The Action:**

- The AI doesn't just "store" them; it **Self-Heals** the data—automatically tagging them, identifying outdated info, and linking them to existing projects in the Knowledge Graph.

### Gap Analysis (Competitive Edge)

**The Logic:**

- If you're building a business plan, the AI scans your "Knowledge Hub" and says: _"You have a marketing strategy, but no budget for 'AWS Hosting' mentioned in your tech notes. Should I create a budget gap task?"_

---

## 2. The Editor: "The Command Center"

> The editor is no longer for writing; it's for **executing**. It uses a **"Slash" (/)** command system designed for Agentic AI.

### Action Extraction (The Core Shift)

- As you type, a **"Subtle Highlight"** appears over phrases like _"Check with the team on Docker progress."_
- **One-Click Task:** Clicking the highlight instantly creates a task in the Team Action Board and pings the relevant person via the AI-Tagging system.

### Source Transparency (Replacing Citations)

- Every AI-generated summary or task has a **"Digital Fingerprint."**
- Hover over a sentence to see a small preview of the exact internal file or Slack message it was derived from.
- _Trust is the 2026 gold standard._

### Shareable "Action Hubs" (The Monetization Bridge)

- Instead of a boring shared folder, you create a **"Hub"** where the AI acts as a Project Manager.

**The Hybrid Model:**
| Tier | Model |
|------|-------|
| Individual | Free |
| Team | Per-Seat or Usage-Based fee for AI-managed "Action Extraction" and "Gap Analysis" |

---

## 3. The Visual Identity: "Tactile & Spatial"

> Since you love visuals, the 2026 design trend is **Spatial** and **Human-Centric**.

### The UI

- **No more "flat/boring" look**
- **High Contrast** palettes for actions
- **Tactile Textures** (paper-like backgrounds) to make the digital workspace feel like a real laboratory

### The Graph View

- It isn't just a map; it's an **Active Navigation** tool.
- Clicking a node in the graph opens that specific **"Action Hub"** or **"Command Center"** editor.
